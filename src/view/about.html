About Me

With extensive experience in education, I've successfully guided students to excel in advanced mathematics. Simultaneously, I enjoy leveraging Python scripting for optimizing processes. My competitive drive fuels my enthusiasm for competitive programming, where I actively participate with my team in prestigious contests such as the ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest) and others. I am constantly driven to learn and improve, pushing the boundaries of what's possible at the intersection of math and technology to foster innovation in every project.
